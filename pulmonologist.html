Who is Pulmonologist?

Pulmonology is a specialized medical field that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of diseases and conditions that impact the respiratory system. A pulmonologist is a highly skilled physician who possesses in-depth knowledge and expertise in preventing, diagnosing, and treating various lung diseases and disorders. Condition We Treated As Pulmonologist?

  • Dizziness, lightheadedness or fainting
  • Difficulty breathing, especially during    exercise
  • Fatigue
